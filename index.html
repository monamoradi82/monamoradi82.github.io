About Me

I am an AI Engineer specializing in Machine Learning, Edge AI, and IoT for digital healthcare. My work focuses on developing real-time, on-device intelligence to improve latency, security, and scalability in medical applications.

With a strong foundation in computer architecture, VLSI design, and nanoscale technologies, I bridge advanced hardware research with practical AI solutions. Currently, I am a Research Fellow at Scuola Superiore Sant’Anna in Italy, contributing to AI-driven mobile health projects.

Beyond academia, I enjoy exploring new cultures, music, cooking, and reading historical and philosophical literature.
