About Me

I am an AI Engineer specializing in Machine Learning, Edge AI, and IoT for digital healthcare. My work focuses on developing real-time, on-device intelligence for medical applications, improving latency, security, and scalability across distributed healthcare environments.

Currently, I am involved in designing AI-driven healthcare monitoring systems and secure IoT architectures, while actively contributing to European research and innovation initiatives. I have recently designed and developed an AI-powered mobile health application within the Proximity Care and Tuscany Health Ecosystem (THE) projects, integrating Edge AI, IoT connectivity, and voice-based risk detection.

With over 10 years of experience across academia and industry, I bring a strong background in computer engineering, computer architecture, and hardware design, including VLSI and nanoscale technologies. I am particularly interested in working at the edge of emerging technologies and bridging the gap between advanced research and real-world applications.

Beyond research, I enjoy exploring new places and cultures. I also have a strong interest in historical and novel literature, especially in history and philosophy, where I continuously seek new perspectives and insights.
